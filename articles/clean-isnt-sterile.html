“Sterile” is an anxiety fantasy. It’s what happens when marketing meets shame. You can’t scrub a living system into trust. You can only disrupt it until it complains.

comfort is the honest metric

Comfort is how the body votes on your routine. If your products burn, tighten, tingle, or perfume the truth away, you’re not caring — you’re managing fear.

The body doesn’t need punishment to be worthy of closeness.

what “clean” actually looks like

  • external-only washing
  • gentle ingredients
  • less friction
  • time and rest

what “sterile” tries to sell you

  • over-washing
  • internal cleaning
  • strong fragrance
  • deodorizing claims
