Best unscented moisturizers (for external comfort)

Moisturizer should calm skin, not perfume it. The best options are unscented, simple, and designed to support the barrier.

  • Skin
  • External care
  • Comfort
  • Aftercare

what to look for

  • unscented / fragrance-free
  • simple ingredient list
  • no “cooling” additives
  • absorbs without leaving sticky residue

what to avoid

  • heavy fragrance or essential oils
  • menthol / “tingle” sensations
  • products that burn on contact

If you apply it and immediately think “oh no,” believe that feeling.

how to use it

  • external only
  • small amount
  • apply after pat-drying
  • stop if irritation increases
