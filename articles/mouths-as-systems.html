Mouths as systems

A mouth isn’t a tool. It’s a living system — temperature, moisture, pressure, time. The best outcomes come from restraint and responsiveness.

Diagram: timing → moisture → gentleness.

What matters most

  • Moisture: dryness creates friction; friction creates irritation.
  • Pressure: consistent and gentle beats aggressive and inconsistent.
  • Time: rushed technique reads as performance, not care.

What to avoid

  • Alcohol-based mouthwash right before (irritation risk).
  • “Flavor” products that sting, numb, or tingle.
  • Overly scrubby brushing right before (micro-irritation).
This is not a “trick list.” It’s systems etiquette.
