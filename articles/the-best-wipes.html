The best wipes

The best wipes don’t try to improve anything. They clean externally, gently, and then get out of the way.

  • External care
  • Wipes
  • Aftercare

Wipes are often sold as solutions — for odor, for discomfort, for things that are framed as problems. In reality, most bodies just need a light reset on the outside.

What wipes are actually for

Wipes are useful for removing sweat, residue, or fluids from skin. That’s it. They’re not meant to disinfect, deodorize, or “freshen” anything internal.

When wipes do too much, they tend to create the very irritation they’re marketed to prevent.

What to look for

  • Unscented or very lightly scented
  • Short ingredient lists
  • Designed for external skin use only

What to avoid

  • Heavy fragrance or “cooling” agents
  • Alcohol-forward formulas
  • Marketing language that promises transformation

A good wipe leaves skin feeling neutral — not tingly, tight, or scented.
