Skin as a system

Skin isn’t a surface you dominate. It’s a barrier system. Most irritation comes from too much input — not neglect.

The basic truth

Skin wants stability. It does not want novelty, punishment, or daily experiments.

What helps the system

  • Gentle cleansing — not “squeaky clean.”
  • Simple moisture — boring beats clever.
  • Time — the system needs consistency.

What disrupts it

  • Fragrance, scrubs, and tingling sensations.
  • Layering too many “actives.”
  • Changing routines every few days.
If your routine feels exciting, it’s probably too much.
