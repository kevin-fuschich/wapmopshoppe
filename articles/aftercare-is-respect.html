Aftercare is respect

Aftercare isn’t optional. It’s how intimacy ends without leaving someone alone in it.

What aftercare actually is

It’s not a checklist. It’s attentiveness. Water. Warmth. Checking in.

Why it matters

Bodies shift after intimacy — physically and emotionally. Respect is noticing that shift and responding.

After doesn’t mean absent.

What it can look like

  • A glass of water.
  • Staying present instead of immediately disengaging.
  • Making space instead of vanishing.
