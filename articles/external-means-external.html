External means external

This shouldn’t be controversial — but it is. External care stops at the outside.

The misunderstanding

Many products blur the line between “support” and “interference.” When instructions get vague, systems get disrupted.

The rule

If a body part is self-cleaning, your job is to leave it alone.

What external care looks like

  • Warm water.
  • Gentle contact.
  • No penetration disguised as hygiene.

Why this matters

Most irritation cycles begin with good intentions and bad boundaries.
