Aftercare

After doesn’t mean absent. This collection is about comfort, cleanup, hydration, and staying human once the moment ends.

What we’re selecting for

  • Simple cleanup that doesn’t disrupt ecosystems
  • Comfort items that reduce friction and irritation
  • Basics that support connection (not performance)
  • Products that feel neutral, not scented or theatrical

What we’re excluding

  • Anything that implies shame or “fixing” a body
  • Harsh wipes, heavy fragrance, aggressive ingredients
  • Products that turn aftercare into another routine to win
The best aftercare usually looks like water, warmth, and presence.

Recommendations will live here. We’ll start with a few quiet essentials — and keep it short.
