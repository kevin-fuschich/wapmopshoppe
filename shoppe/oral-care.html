Oral care

Mouths are ecosystems too. This collection favors gentle maintenance, hydration, and comfort — not harshness, “burn,” or chemical bravado.

What we’re selecting for

  • Gentle, daily-use basics
  • Hydration support (especially for dryness)
  • Non-irritating ingredients and clear intent
  • Clean breath without turning your mouth into a desert

What we’re excluding

  • Products that rely on “sting” or “burn” to feel effective
  • Overly strong whitening routines as a default
  • Anything that leaves the mouth chronically dry

Recommendations will live here. We’ll add a short list of the basics first — then only expand if it’s truly necessary.
