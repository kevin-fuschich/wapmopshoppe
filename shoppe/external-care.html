External care

The goal is comfort with low interference. External means external. Choose boring, gentle tools and avoid “tingle,” heavy fragrance, and anything that turns normal skin into a science project.

Wipes (boring on purpose)

Look for fragrance-free, alcohol-free, and “sensitive” positioning. The win is clean + calm, not perfumed.

Gentle wash

External skin likes mild cleansers. If it burns, tingles, or dries you out, it’s not “working” — it’s irritating.
